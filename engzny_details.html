Engzny

Overview

Engzny provides a variety of home services, including carpentry, plumbing, and electrical work, among others. It provides skilled and experienced professionals who can provide reliable and efficient service for homeowners. The website has an easy-to-use interface, transparent pricing, a satisfaction guarantee, and highly skilled service providers who are licensed, and background-checked. Customers can book services online and choose the best time for the service to be performed.

Problem

As we know, there is no home that is free of malfunctions, whether related to electricity, plumbing, carpentry, electrical appliances, or many other things, and it takes so much time to look for a technician or craftsman to fix the malfunction. In the past, if we wanted to repair something related to electricity, plumbing, carpentry, or any other thing, we needed to search for a technician or craftsman, and it's never been easy to find a guaranteed one that masters his work. It took so much time and effort to just find any technician, and eventually he might be busy during your free time or unavailable

Solution

make a 24-hour web application available with highly qualified guaranteed technicians and save you a lot of time and effort. We can also help technicians get more tasks daily so we can help reduce the percentage of unemployment.

This web application connects homeowners with skilled and experienced professionals who can provide reliable and efficient service for all of their home maintenance and repair needs

Technologies & Tools
ASP.Net Core MVC, Repositorty Pattren, Razor Pages, SignalR, AutoMapper, Dependancy Injection, Datatables, JQuery, and Bootstrap.
